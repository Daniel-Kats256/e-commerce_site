Privacy Policy

1. Introduction,

Welcome to Flip Avenue. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect the personal information of our clients, customers, and website visitors.By accessing our website and using our services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.


2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal information:

(a) Personal Identifiers: Name, contact information (email address, phone number, physical address).
(b) Financial Information: Billing details, payment information.
(c) Commercial Information: Records of products or services purchased, obtained, or considered.
(d) Usage Data: Information on how you use our website and services.
(e) Correspondence: Correspondence and communications exchanged with us.


3. How We Use Your Information.

(a) Providing and managing services you've requeste
(b) Responding to inquiries and providing customer support.
(c) Analyzing and improving our website, services, and user experience.

4.Disclosure of Information

We may share your information with:

(a) Service Providers: Third-party providers who help us deliver services, such as payment processors, shipping companies, and IT service providers.
