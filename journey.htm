Our Story

About Us

Established in 2020, Flip Avenue Limited emerged from a creative synergy, bringing together professionals passionate about redefining interior design in Uganda. Founded by dedicated individuals in the architectural and engineering fields, the studio was conceived to seamlessly blend aesthetics and functionality, leaving an indelible imprint on the design landscape. With over a decade of experience, Flip Avenue specializes in interior and architectural design, offering a comprehensive range of services, including remodeling, custom furnishings, space planning, style design, landscape design, and project management. The approach emphasizes effective space planning, personalized style designs, and eco-conscious landscape designs. Seasoned project managers meticulously oversee every project, ensuring timelines and budgets are adhered to. Flip Avenue is more than a design studio; it's a trusted partner committed to transforming spaces into captivating and functional works of art, driven by creativity and an unwavering pursuit of excellence. .

Mission

To craft exceptional havens by seamlessly integrating high-quality innovation and design to offer our clients timeless and unparalleled experiences.

Vision
